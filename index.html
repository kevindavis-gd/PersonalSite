My name is Kevin Davis and I was born on a small Caribbean island called Grenada. As a young child, I was always interested in artificial intelligence and robotics. Removing and tinkering with the internals of radios and televisions was the joy of my childhood. This led to my pursuance of a bachelor's degree in computer science at the Midwestern State University; my expected graduation date is May 2021. After graduation I hope to start my career as a software engineer.

Alongside my studies, I have gained further experience working with professionals as a CRM System Administrator at Midwestern State University. I have become a valued part of the CRM team because of my focus on practical solutions to issues with IT systems. My technical skills include extensive knowledge of programming, math, algorithms, and coding languages; these include C++, Python, HTML, and CSS.

I am a positive, motivated, and hard-working person who is keen to learn and contribute. My technical skills are combined with excellent communication skills that allow me to work effectively and positively, and get along well with others. I truly believe in continued education and research. I strive to seek new information daily to contribute to my growth.
